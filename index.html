I am a botanist with many skills and competences in ethno pharmacology, environmental sciences, biodiversity conservation, climate change and teaching

Vision

A sound and healthy society resilient to climate change effects and other crisis through conservation and sustainable use of natural resources

Mission

• Promoting community health and development by connecting people, ideas and resources • Producing and widely disseminate knowledge on the conservation, processing and use of key natural resources responding to urgent needs (food security, health, poverty alleviation…) • Conducting research based advocacy for improving the framework of policies, institutions and laws related to the sustainable management of natural resources. • Promoting a society free from any sort of pollution, discrimination and violence derived from the bad governance and sharing of natural resources. etc.

Objectives

To improve the connection of people, ideas and resources for community health and development. To foster the conservation and sustainable use of key natural resources responding to immediate needs (food, health, water…) at local level. To conduction environmental and social impact assessment To reduce the pollution, discrimination, violence and bad governance over the management of natural resources. To improve community resilience to climate change and other hazards. To produce efficient improved traditional medicines worldwide To realize environmental and social impact

Foundamental problem

The planet is highly exploited and life is increasingly threatened. The socio-economic development of the world without respect for the pillars of sustainable development (social equity, economic efficiency and environmental health) creates social and economic inequalities, at all levels of human society. It impacts the earth irreversibly. While this has become at least 5 to 6 times insufficient to meet human needs is deteriorating every day. It is imperative to save the earth and life (human, animal and plant)
